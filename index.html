About Revolution Human

At Revolution Human Physical Therapy and Education we love to help people in their 40’s, 50’s, 60’s and above, get back to living the life they deserve. Which means a life free from painkillers, where you don’t have to worry about bothering the doctor, and can do and enjoy all of the things you love, without the fear of pain holding you back.

A Better Experience

Patient First

We take the time to listen to you, to hear you, and include you in your care.

Building a relationship

We strive to create a strong patient/therapist alliance to make sure you will be at your best.

Honesty

We will do our best to be honest about all aspects of your care including prices and also make sure that we provide the knowledge for you to feel like you are in control.

Transparency

We will strive to be fully transparent in all areas including interventions, treatment process and knowledge.
